<p><strong>The Industrial Grade Optical Network Unit Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Active Optical Network Unit</li><li>Passive Optical Network Unit</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Industrial Grade Optical Network Unit (ONU) market consists of two primary types: Active Optical Network Units (AONUs) and Passive Optical Network Units (PONUs). AONUs use powered electronics to convert and amplify signals for long-distance transmission, allowing for high-speed data transfer and flexibility in network design. In contrast, PONUs rely on passive optical components, utilizing splitters to distribute signals, resulting in lower operational costs and energy efficiency. Both types cater to varying industrial connectivity needs and application scenarios.</p></p>
