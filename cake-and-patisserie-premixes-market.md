<p><ul><li>Complete mix</li><li>Dough-base mix</li><li>Dough concentrates</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cake and patisserie premixes are categorized into three main types: complete mix, dough-base mix, and dough concentrates. Complete mixes contain all necessary ingredients for cake or pastry preparation, requiring minimal additional input. Dough-base mixes provide a foundation for various baked goods but need further ingredients for customization. Dough concentrates are highly concentrated formulations that require dilution with water or other ingredients before use. Each type simplifies the baking process, catering to different levels of convenience and customization for bakers.</p></p>
