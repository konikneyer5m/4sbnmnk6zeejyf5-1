<p><p>The organic vegetable powders market is divided into two main types: feed grade and food grade. Feed grade powders are specifically formulated for animal consumption, enhancing nutritional value in livestock feed and pet food. They offer concentrated nutrients, flavors, and colors, catering to health-conscious consumers and the culinary industry.</p></p>
