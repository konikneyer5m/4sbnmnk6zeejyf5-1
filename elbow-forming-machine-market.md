<p><p>The elbow forming machine market encompasses two primary types: elbow cold forming machines and elbow hot forming machines. Elbow cold forming machines utilize mechanical processes to shape materials at room temperature, ensuring high precision and minimal material waste. In contrast, elbow hot forming machines apply heat to soften materials, allowing for more significant deformation and complex shapes. Both types cater to diverse industries, including construction and manufacturing, with specific applications based on the required material properties and production volumes.</p></p>
